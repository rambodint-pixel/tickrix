03 / The Method

Three steps.
Sixty seconds.

— Step 01

Plug in.

USB-C to any charger, laptop, or monitor port. Tickrix boots in 3 seconds and broadcasts a setup network.

— Step 02

Connect.

Join "Tickrix-Setup" on your phone. Setup page opens automatically. Pick your home Wi-Fi. Save. Tickrix remembers it forever.

— Step 03

Watch.

Pick your tickers. Choose any exchange. Live prices appear. Set it on your desk. Get back to work.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026 · Tickrix® B.V. · Rotterdam, NL

This Privacy Policy explains what data Tickrix® B.V. ("Tickrix", "we", "us") collects when you visit tickrix.com, join our waitlist, or use a Tickrix device. We are based in Rotterdam, Netherlands and comply with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you join the waitlist or contact us. Used to notify you about Edition One launch and product updates.
  • Device configuration — your selected exchange and tickers, stored under a random Device ID. This is the only data your Tickrix sends to our servers.
  • Anonymous analytics — page views, country (not city), browser type. No personal identifiers, no tracking across sites.

What we never collect

  • Your Wi-Fi password — it stays on the device, never on our servers.
  • Your trading account credentials, wallets, or balances.
  • Your real name, address, or location data unless you explicitly provide it.

Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, email hello@tickrix.com. We respond within 30 days.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for advertising. We use Supabase (EU servers) for backend storage and Vercel for hosting — both GDPR-compliant. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar tracking tools.
