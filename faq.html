— About the product
What is Tickrix, exactly?

Tickrix is a WiFi-connected market display for traders. It's a 1.91-inch AMOLED screen housed in CNC-milled aluminum, designed to sit on your desk and show the live price of any market you care about — crypto, forex, stocks, gold, oil, indices.

Plug it in, connect to WiFi, pick your asset. That's it. One device. One purpose. Beautifully done.

How is it different from competitors?

Three real differences:

1. Build quality. Most market tickers are plastic and cheap. Tickrix is CNC-milled aluminum, built to last a decade.

2. The base. No competitor includes a matching aluminum stand. Yours does, with calibrated 20° viewing angle.

3. Coverage. Most show only crypto. Tickrix shows everything — including forex with real 5-decimal pip precision.

What markets does it support?

10,000+ instruments across all major asset classes:

Crypto: BTC, ETH, SOL, and 8,000+ pairs from CoinGecko, Binance, Bybit.
Forex: Major and minor pairs with 5-decimal pip precision (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, etc.).
Stocks: US, EU, UK exchanges.
Commodities: Gold, Silver, Oil, Natural Gas.
Indices: S&P 500, NASDAQ, DAX, FTSE, Nikkei.

Do I need to install an app?

No. Setup happens through any browser via WiFi captive portal. Connect to "Tickrix-Setup", configure your assets, save. Done.

No App Store. No Play Store. No account required.

— Technical
Does it have a battery?

Two configurations:

Wired (default): USB-C powered, always-on, perfect for permanent desk placement.
Battery option: 500mAh lithium polymer, 4-6 hours runtime, charges via USB-C.

What WiFi does it need?

Standard 2.4 GHz WiFi. Works with home routers, office WiFi, and most mobile hotspots. No 5GHz support to keep power consumption low.

How often does the price update?

Every 5 to 60 seconds, configurable. Default is 30 seconds — fast enough to feel real-time, slow enough to be respectful of public APIs and your battery.

Can I change what's displayed?

Yes. Through the Tickrix dashboard (no app needed), you can swap assets anytime. Mix crypto, forex, and stocks. Set up rotating displays. Customize text colors. It's your device.
