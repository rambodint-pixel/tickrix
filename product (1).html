OUR STORY

WHY WE
BUILT THIS.

THE PROBLEM

You are always looking at your phone.

As a trader, you know the feeling. You are in the middle of something — a meeting, a conversation, a meal — and you reach for your phone to check prices. Again. And again.

Bloomberg terminals cost $24,000 per year. Phone apps are distracting. Browser tabs get buried. There was nothing in between — a simple, dedicated display that just shows you the markets. Always. Without the noise.

THE SOLUTION

A desk display built from scratch.

We are a small team from Rotterdam, Netherlands. We trade. We build. And we got tired of reaching for our phones every five minutes.

So we built Tickrix — a WiFi-connected AMOLED display that sits on your desk and shows live prices for crypto, stocks, gold and oil. No subscription. No distractions. Just the data you need, always visible, always live.

We showed the first prototype to an 86-year-old retired trader with 40 years of experience. His response? "If it works, €100 is nothing for that device." That was all we needed to hear.
