01 — Who we are.

Tickrix ("we", "us", "our") is a brand operated by an individual entrepreneur based in Rotterdam, Netherlands. This Privacy Policy applies to tickrix.com and all related services.

For privacy matters, contact us at hello@tickrix.com.

02 — What we collect.

Information you give us directly

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or contact us
  • Name and message content — when you use the contact form
  • Order information — name, shipping address, payment details (post-launch only)

Information collected automatically

  • Anonymous usage data — page views, browser type, country (IP address rounded to country)
  • Device data from purchased units — only what's needed for live data fetching (no personal identifiers)

What we DON'T collect

  • We don't track you across other websites
  • We don't sell your data to anyone, ever
  • We don't use ad networks or third-party tracking pixels
  • We don't read your purchases or trading activity

03 — How we use it.

We only use your information for these purposes:

  • Sending waitlist updates — when Tickrix launches and ships
  • Responding to your messages — when you contact us directly
  • Fulfilling orders — shipping, payment processing, customer support (post-launch)
  • Improving the website — anonymous, aggregated analytics only
  • Legal compliance — when required by law

04 — Legal basis (GDPR).

Under EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your personal data based on:

  • Consent (Art. 6.1.a) — when you sign up to the waitlist or send a message
  • Contract performance (Art. 6.1.b) — when fulfilling orders
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f) — for basic website analytics
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6.1.c) — when required by tax or commercial law

05 — Who we share it with.

We share data only with trusted third-party processors who help us operate Tickrix:

  • Supabase — secure database hosting (waitlist emails)
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Payment processors (post-launch) — Stripe or equivalent
  • Shipping carriers (post-launch) — DHL, FedEx, etc.

All processors are GDPR-compliant. We never sell, rent, or trade your data with anyone for marketing purposes.

06 — How long we keep it.

  • Waitlist emails: Until you unsubscribe or 24 months of inactivity
  • Contact form messages: 24 months after last interaction
  • Order data: 7 years (Dutch tax law requirement)
  • Analytics data: 14 months, then anonymized

07 — Your rights.

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction — limit how we process your data
  • Right to portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to object — object to processing for marketing
  • Right to withdraw consent — at any time, without penalty

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@tickrix.com. We respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) at autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl.

08 — Cookies.

Tickrix.com uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies — for basic site function (no consent needed)
  • Analytics cookies — anonymous, aggregated only

We do NOT use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party social media trackers.

09 — Security.

We take security seriously. All data is transmitted over HTTPS with TLS 1.3 encryption. Passwords (where applicable) are hashed with bcrypt. Our database is encrypted at rest.

Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure. If we ever experience a data breach affecting your personal data, we'll notify you within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

10 — Changes to this policy.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. For significant changes, we'll notify you by email if we have your address.

11 — Contact us.

Questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy:

Mail: Tickrix, Rotterdam, Netherlands
Response time: Within 5 business days for general inquiries, 30 days for GDPR requests.
